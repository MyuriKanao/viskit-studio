# Step 3 — Hero images (H1-H5)

Output exactly 5 hero-image briefs, **strictly ordered H1, H2, H3, H4, H5**:

- H1 First-impression CTR driver: product centered, background mood, motion hook
- H2 Pain-point empathy: problem scene vs. solution (split composition)
- H3 Differentiator vs. competitors: side-by-side product shots or data visual
- H4 Scenario fit / use value: 2-3 lifestyle scenes (triptych)
- H5 CTA call-to-action: full product lineup + CTA button area

Each hero only carries three pieces: **Visual / Copy / Design Note**.
Copy ≤5 lines; headline ≤10 chars; subhead ≤15 chars.
